About this cottage

Meadow View Cottage is a beautifully restored 19th-century stone cottage nestled on the edge of Keswick, with stunning views across Derwentwater and the surrounding fells.

Recently refurbished to a high standard, the cottage blends original character — exposed beams, stone fireplaces — with modern comforts including underfloor heating and a fully equipped kitchen.

The cottage is perfectly placed for walking, cycling, and exploring the Lake District. Keswick town centre is a 10-minute walk, with pubs, restaurants, and the famous Pencil Museum nearby.
